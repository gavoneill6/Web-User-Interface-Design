Tadhg O'Halloran (b. 1986), is an Irish photographer based in Cork, where he studied photography at St. Johns College. His work tends to be personal documentary-style black-and-white photographs of his friends, hometown and landscapes. He has had various solo and group exhibitions in both Rome and Cork. He built and is currently running a darkroom in Cork City that is shared with other photographers and artists.

The photos on this website form part of a series of photographs where Tadhg tries to define moments of life as he saw it. They are an attempt of forming the world around into photographs and to offer a glimpse of something that he perceived.
